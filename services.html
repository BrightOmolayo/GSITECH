Services

We build the capacity of Business Development Service Providers (BDSPs) through hands-on mentoring and strategic coaching. Our approach enhances their ability to support agribusinesses, drive innovation, and create sustainable impact across rural and urban value chains.

Through AWEIN, we empower women in agriculture with tools, training, and resources to grow thriving agribusinesses. We champion inclusive development by bridging gender gaps and supporting female-led innovation in farming, processing, and agritrade.

We connect producers to profitable markets by facilitating value chain integration, product standardization, and buyer-seller relationships. Our market linkage support ensures smallholders and agripreneurs access better pricing, consistent demand, and long-term growth opportunities.

Our poultry and aquaculture interventions combine technical expertise with community-driven models. We provide training, input support, and sustainable technologies to boost production, enhance food safety, and build resilience among local producers.

Our internship program nurtures the next generation of agri-professionals. By offering field experience, mentorship, and hands-on learning, we equip young talents with the skills needed to thrive in Nigeria’s growing agricultural economy.

We design and deliver practical, demand-driven training sessions tailored to various actors in the agricultural value chain. From farm management to agritech adoption, our trainings are rooted in real-world relevance and impact.

We engage, educate, and empower youth through skills development, entrepreneurship training, and awareness campaigns. Our programs inspire young people to see agriculture as a viable, modern, and rewarding career path.

We offer expert consultancy services to governments, NGOs, and private-sector players. Our strengths lie in agribusiness development, value chain analysis, project implementation, and policy advisory—driving scalable, evidence-based solutions.

From feasibility studies to operational support, we help agribusinesses plan and manage for growth. Our integrated services include business modeling, resource optimization, and strategic planning tailored to the agricultural landscape.
