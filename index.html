On-going Activities

Training Activities

Since the onset of year 2015, we began 'selling' our capacity building services which targeted producers, processors and agro (input) dealers within 2 major Agric value chain commodities namely cassava and aquaculture. Currently, we included the Poultry value chain and have a growing network of poultry farmers and processors in Ondo and Oyo States indicating interest in receiving technical and enterprise training.

Internship Program

We run an internship program where undergraduate students, youth corps members and post-NYSC graduates interested in learning specific skills in agriculture (hatching, breeding or processing), various crops management, finished leather goods etc are enrolled. Interns learn to engage technical skills along with enterprise skills to enable them make strategic business decisions

Livestock and Agribusiness

In 2016, we launched a hands-on, experiential training tool in Ondo State, designed to build real-world skills for producers, processors, and marketers across the livestock value chain. This applied learning approach is tailored to boost productivity, improve processing efficiency, and enhance market access — creating stronger, more profitable agribusinesses. Investing in people means investing in performance. This pilot sets the stage for scalable impact in Nigeria’s livestock sector.
