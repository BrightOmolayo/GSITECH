On-going Activities

Training Activities

Since the onset of year 2015, we began 'selling' our capacity building services which targeted producers, processors and agro (input) dealers within 2 major Agric value chain commodities namely cassava and aquaculture. In 2016, we included the Poultry value chain and have a growing network of poultry farmers and processors in Ondo and Oyo States indicating interest in receiving technical and enterprise training.

Internship Program

We run an internship program where undergraduate students, youth corps members and post-NYSC graduates interested in learning specific skills in aquaculture (hatching, breeding or processing), various crops management, finished leather goods etc are enrolled. Interns learn to engage technical skills along with enterprise skills to enable them make strategic business decisions

Poultry & Aquaculture

The technical and enterprise training units are currently working to develop/adapt an experiential based training tool centered on applied learning and tailored to build the capacity of producers, processors and marketers within the poultry value chain. The pilot implementation of this tool shall flag off this year 2016 in Ondo State.
